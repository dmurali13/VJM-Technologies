Our Events

View All Posts

The School Play or Drama Production involves students in acting, directing, stage management, and costume design, offering them an opportunity to immerse themselves in the performing arts. Whether it's a classic work or a modern play, this event showcases students' artistic talents and hard work, allowing them to perform in front of their peers, teachers, and parents.

The Talent Show gives students a chance to showcase their unique talents outside of the classroom. Whether it's singing, dancing, acting, magic, or any other skill, this event is designed to celebrate the creativity and diversity of students. It's an opportunity for students to build confidence, entertain their peers, and support each other in a fun and supportive environment.
